Ninth Grade


Requirements:


Going to high school in California means that you need to pay attention to the A-G requirements. These requirements map out the basic classes you must take (and pass) in order to graduate from high school. In ninth grade, you should be taking World History (honors or AP, depending on your school and the amount of workload you want to have), English 9 (honors or regular), Algebra I or Geometry (based on the last math you took in eighth grade), Biology, and a Language Other Than English I or II (based on what you took in eighth grade).

This summer you should take or consider taking Health. It is required by many schools in California to complete this class in order to graduate high school.
