Eighth Grade


Requirements:


For eighth grade, you don't need to worry too much about which classes you're taking. In most middle schools, your counselor will sort you into the required classes. What you do need to think about though in terms of your class, is whether or not you want to take a Language Other Than English (in some places LOTE). Here at WEBSITE NAME we recommend taking a Language Other Than English in eighth grade, especially if you're planning on going to a four year college straight out of high school. If your school doesn't offer any language classes, or if you're in eighth grade now and weren't put into a language class, don't worry! You can still fulfill the requirement in high school. Taking a Language Other Than English in eighth grade helps you fulfill the requirement faster and make room for more classes in your high school schedule.
