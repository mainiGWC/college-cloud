AP stands for Advanced Placement. Taking these tests, through Collegeboard, can help prove that you are capable of handling rigorous coursework and eliminate some undergraduate level courses in unversity. You can take AP courses as soon as your freshman year in high school where your teachers help you prepare for the exams. You may also qualify for a fee waiver for your AP exam depending on your financial need. Collegeboard will reduce the fee by $33 for students that are eligible.

There is no exact number of how many AP exams you should take. Here is a list of some of most common AP exams offered through CollegeBoard:
