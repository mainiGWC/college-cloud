Testing: AP's, SAT, ACT

What is an AP test?



AP stands for Advanced Placement. Taking these tests, through Collegeboard, can help prove that you are capable of handling rigorous coursework and eliminate some undergraduate level courses in unversity. You can take AP courses as soon as your freshman year in high school where your teachers help you prepare for the exams. You may also qualify for a fee waiver for your AP exam depending on your financial need. Collegeboard will reduce the fee by $33 for students that are eligible.

    I am interested. Where do I start?



    Visit CollegeBoard and create a username and password. Keep track of your login information because CollegeBoard is the hub for any testing related information (AP, SAT, ACT). To register for an exam, you need to sign into your AP classroom in Collegeboard. Your school will walk you through the steps of which AP exam you should be registered for. Please note that many schools require you to specify, which exam you are taking in November, even though the exam is in May. More information can be found here

    Khan Academy


    Khan Academy is one of the best study tools avaliable to all stuents for free. They have everything you need: from AP practice questions to life skills. If there is a particular subject you have a hard time with, go through the subject on Khan Academy the summer before you take the course, and you will be better prepared. Highly recommended!



    Check it out!

    Barrons/Princeton Review


    Check to see if your local library has any AP exam preparation materials for you to check out. Both Barrons and Princeton Review are groups that publish preparation books with practice tests and tips as well as flashcards. They are very effective for studying and cover all parts of the test.

    Teachers



    Sometimes the best resources are your teachers. Don't be afraid to reach out to them. They are there to help you learn and be successful. For AP exam preparation, your teachers may have, or know where you can find, practice AP exam questions.
