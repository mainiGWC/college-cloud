Twelfth Grade


Requirements:


In twelfth grade, you should be taking Government (honors or AP), English 12 (honors or AP), Pre-Calculus or Calculus (based on the last math you took in eleventh grade), a science of your choice (honors or AP), a Language Other Than English IV or (based on what you took before) or an elective/AP class of your choice, and Economics (h)

You are only required to take 3 years of math, or as many years of math that gets you to Algebra II. We highly recommend taking 4 years of math or all the way to Calculus. Not only does it increase your competitiveness for college, but it allows you to gain more of an understanding of more kinds of math, which can help you later in college.

In terms of the A-G requirements, only 2 years of a Language Other Than English is required. If your school offers more levels of the language you have chosen to take, we at WEBSITE NAME recommend taking a 3rd or 4th year of the class. Taking more years of a Language Other Than English increases your competitiveness for college as well as allows you to become fluent in another language.
